What exactly are sleeping disorders?

To put it simply, a sleeping disorder is a medical condition that affects an individuals health, safety and quality of life. These conditions are very real medical problems that affect millions worldwide and can in some cases have dire consequences.

Why do you need a sleep diagnosis?

The benefits of good sleep are simpler to understand, compared to the actual concept of sleep. A good night's sleep is essential to feel fresh and active in the morning and during the entire day. It is no surprise that sleeping has always constituted a significant part of our lifestyle, since time immemorial. Since sleep plays one of the most important roles in our lives, anything that disrupts a peaceful sleep is considered a problem. Snoring is one factor that is as a hindrance to a good night's sleep, and therefore, has been widely studied and understood.
